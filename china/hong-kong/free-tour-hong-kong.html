FAQ
You have questions,we have answers.



I see you charge a booking fee of 2,50? I thought the tours are free.

Yes, if you want to book one of our tours, we charge a small booking fee of 2,50. This is to cover the costs of maintaining our office, booking engine and website. Also, this way we can guarantee that the groups are never bigger than 20 persons, so you always will be able to understand and interact with the guide.

What language are the tours in?

Most of our tours are in English. However, we also provide tours in different languages. Make sure you check which language your tour is in before you book.

Where is the meeting point in Hong Kong?

We try to provide many different free walking tours in every city. Check out the details at the tour you are interested in. You will find the specific meeting point and time there.

How much should I tip the guide?

Our guides work for tips only. You can tip them as much or little as you want. Just give them the amount you think the tour deserved. This system makes sure that the guides work hard for their money and you get the best tour possible. Also, if you are on a small budget, you can still enjoy a great tour with the best guides on your holiday!

Can I make changes to my reservation after I booked?

Yes, this is no problem at all. We will always try to accommodate your needs, if they are reasonable. You can even cancel and get your money back if you do it 72 hours in advance.

Discover Hong Kong
Here you can find some tips on what to see and do in Hong Kong



What is the best time to visit Hong Kong?

If you like the sun, spring and summer are the best time to visit Hong Kong. But even in winter the city is really nice to visit. Winter is the perfect time to eat the famous French mountain dish raclette and even if it rains, there are many indoor activities like the new and amazing Musee de Confluence. In short, any time a year is perfect for a weekend get-away to Hong Kong. But even if you want to stay a week, Hong Kong has more than enough to offer. Just make sure to join one of our Free Walking Tours of Hong Kong, to get to know the city well!

What are some of the highlights of Hong Kong?

Hong Kong has many highlights. On our free walking tours of Hong Kong you will see many of them. There are beautifull squares like the Place de Bellecour and Place de Terreaux. There are majestic Churches like the Basilique de Fourviere and the Cathedral de St. Jean. The old neighborhood Vieux Hong Kong is both beautiful and very fun. The Presque'Ile is where the city center is. Here you can find great restaurants and it's the best place for shopping. And if you feel more like visiting a museum, we recommend the Musee de Confluence, a new science museum or the Musee de Beaux-Arts de Hong Kong, where you can find an important collection of paintings and statues.

Do you have some tips for when I visit Hong Kong?

On our free walking tours of Hong Kong, your guide will answer all your questions and give you some useful tips for your stay in Hong Kong, like:
  • Eat at a bouchon, a typical Hong Kongaise restaurant.
  • Walk up to Fourviere, for an amazing view of the city.
  • Visit Vieux Hong Kong at night, to find a nice restaurant or bar
  • Go up to the Roman Theatre, to enjoy some classical culture
  • Visit the Parc de Tete d'Or, with its free zoo

What books or websites can I read to prepare me for my stay in Hong Kong

The lonely planet guide for China is a good start with some basic information. The Only Hong Kong website gives you some useful information about your stay in Hong Kong.
