Get to know our Guides

Our guides love showing you their cities and countries. Join a tour and meet them!

  1. Home
  2. AiroTour's Guides

Our Free Tour Policy guarantees that you will get only the very best guides.

We carefully select our guides. They need to be informative, fun and know their stuff. While many have years of experience working in tourism, others are just starting. For us, the only thing that is important is enthousiasm and the ability to tell a story. We want our guides to bring your destinations alive!

We also believe that our Free Tour Concept will quickly filter out guides that perform poorly, because if they do, they won't get paid! So join us on one of our free tours and get to know our guides. You will see for yourself that a free tour gets you the best guide and the most for your money!

  • Selecting guides

    We are always looking for new guides. However, we don't just hire anybody. We need a potential guide to be able to add great value to a tour. We need them to have a profound understanding of the culture and history of a destination and be familiar with both the economic and political situation of their country and region. This way they will be able to answer any questions you might have. And please, don't hesitate to ask anything. Our guides love to answer your questions.

  • Reviews

    After you did your tour, we sometimes send you an email, asking you to review the tour. We love feedback and like to know what your thoughts were. We are working towards a system where you can put your review automatically on our site, so other customers can see what you liked or disliked about the tour. This way we hope only the very best tours will keep running and the guides will push themselves to provide you with the best experience possible!

  • Tipping

    Our guides work for tips only. You are never ever obliged to tip. All the tips you give the guide go to him or her. We don't take any of their money. We believe this system is the best way to make sure all the guides perform great all the time. If they are boring, they won't get as much money, so they have a clear incentive to be fun!
