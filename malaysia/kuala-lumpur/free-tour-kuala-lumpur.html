Free Tour Kuala Lumpur

Kuala Lumpur knows how to captivate you. It is an amazing mix of modernity and the traditional Malaysian lifestyle. You are surrounded by futuristic skyscrapers, historic monuments and fragrant open air markets. You find traditional mosques around the corner from buzzing nightlife. And the city is green. It is filled with tropical gardens and lush gardens.

In Kuala Lumpur you can find a great cultural mix of different peoples. Trade has long brought prosperity to this colorful city and with it came people from all over the region. Indigenous Malay communities live side by side with Chinese and Indian traders.

This vibrant mix of communities has also created a great mixed Asian cuisine. And food is everywhere in Kuala Lumpur. On one of our Free Tours of Kuala Lumpur, our guides will show you the tastiest food stalls and the best markets. Our guides will show you where to drink the best tea and walk you to all the amazing historic monuments this city has to offer.

Come visit the hustle and bustle of Chinatown with us and truly experience Kuala Lumpur on one of our Free Walking Tours of the city!

Discover Kuala Lumpur
Here you can find some tips on what to see and do in Kuala Lumpur



What is the best time to visit Kuala Lumpur?

If you like the sun, spring and summer are the best time to visit Kuala Lumpur. But even in winter the city is really nice to visit. Winter is the perfect time to eat the famous French mountain dish raclette and even if it rains, there are many indoor activities like the new and amazing Musee de Confluence. In short, any time a year is perfect for a weekend get-away to Kuala Lumpur. But even if you want to stay a week, Kuala Lumpur has more than enough to offer. Just make sure to join one of our Free Walking Tours of Kuala Lumpur, to get to know the city well!

What are some of the highlights of Kuala Lumpur?

Kuala Lumpur has many highlights. On our free walking tours of Kuala Lumpur you will see many of them. There are beautifull squares like the Place de Bellecour and Place de Terreaux. There are majestic Churches like the Basilique de Fourviere and the Cathedral de St. Jean. The old neighborhood Vieux Kuala Lumpur is both beautiful and very fun. The Presque'Ile is where the city center is. Here you can find great restaurants and it's the best place for shopping. And if you feel more like visiting a museum, we recommend the Musee de Confluence, a new science museum or the Musee de Beaux-Arts de Kuala Lumpur, where you can find an important collection of paintings and statues.

Do you have some tips for when I visit Kuala Lumpur?

On our free walking tours of Kuala Lumpur, your guide will answer all your questions and give you some useful tips for your stay in Kuala Lumpur, like:
  • Eat at a bouchon, a typical Kuala Lumpuraise restaurant.
  • Walk up to Fourviere, for an amazing view of the city.
  • Visit Vieux Kuala Lumpur at night, to find a nice restaurant or bar
  • Go up to the Roman Theatre, to enjoy some classical culture
  • Visit the Parc de Tete d'Or, with its free zoo

What books or websites can I read to prepare me for my stay in Kuala Lumpur

The lonely planet guide for Malaysia is a good start with some basic information. The Only Kuala Lumpur website gives you some useful information about your stay in Kuala Lumpur.
