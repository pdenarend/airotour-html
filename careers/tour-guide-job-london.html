We are looking for New Tour Guides in London

Help us find new tour guides in London and get a referral fee of 25€. If you know someone that would be a great tourguide, please send us a message, or let them send us their CV. We are looking for people that love to explain culture and history that is passionate and knows how to tell a story. Maybe he or she is a guide with experience or someone who is willing to learn. They should be able to work by themselves.

AiroTour is a company that provides free tours around the world. We have had a lot of success and are now rapidly expanding. Our guides work for tips only. This often means they get paid more than they would working for a salary.

What are the benefits of becoming a guide?

  • You get to show people from all over your own city
  • You can determine your own hours
  • You are in charge of your own salary
  • The more you work, and the better reviews you get, the more you get to work
  • You meet people from all over the world and make them happy

If you know someone who could be interested, let them send their CV to info@airotour.com. Make sure they provide your email address, so we can contact you to pay you your finders fee directly on your paypal account!

You will get your fee once we hire someone
