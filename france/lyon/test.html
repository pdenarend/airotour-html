Discover all the highlights of Lyon on a Free Tour

Lyon is one of France's most important cities. It is an economic powerhouse in the region and it has a long history. It is also called the culinary capital of France. Many people come from far to eat at one of the traditional 'bouchon', the typical restaurants of the city. But the food is not the only reason to visit Lyon. The city has a beautiful historic center, which can rival with any other French town. You can explore Lyon by booking one of our free tours around the center of Lyon.

One of our free tours of Lyon can be the perfect way to get introduced to this amazing city. Our guides love to show you the most amazing spots in Lyon. You can ask any questions about living in France or the history and culture of the city.

We believe that free walking tours are the best way to enjoy and get to know a city, so book your free tour of Lyon below!

City Tour Lyon

Free walking tour | | 7 reviews

During this city tour, you will get to know all the highlights of the center of Lyon. The walk takes you from Bellecour, the beautiful and enormous square, through Vieux Lyon, the old part of the city. On the way, you will see both the Saone and the Rhone rivers and their picturesque river banks. The walk takes you to the Cathedral de St. Jean and ends at Place Terreaux, the heart of the city center of Lyon.

Highlights

  • Place Bellecour, Saône and Rhône rivers
  • Vieux Lyon, Cathédrake Saint-Jean, Hôtel de Ville
  • Place Terreaux, Jardin du Palais St Pierre, Opéra de Lyon

Useful Info

  • Starting time: 10 am.
  • Meeting point: Place Bellecour
  • Language: English

Vieux Lyon

Free walking tour | | 5 reviews

This free tour explores the old and charming neighborhood of Vieux Lyon. This is the oldest part of Lyon and definitely the neighborhood with the most character. It is located on the river bank of the Saone. Your tour guide will show you all the highlight of the neighborhood, such as the Cathedral St. Jean, the charming Place de la Baleine and the Place de Gouvernement. This area is filled with hidden passageways called 'Traboules', that your guide would love to show you.

Highlights

  • Cathédrale Saint-Jean, Archeological garden Saint-Jean
  • Traboules, La Maison des Avocats, Palais de Justice
  • Place du Gouvernement, Temple du Change, Rue Juiverie

Useful Info

  • Starting time: 15 pm.
  • Meeting point: Place Saint-Jean
  • Language: English

What is the best time to visit Lyon?

If you like the sun, spring and summer are the best time to visit Lyon. But even in winter the city is really nice to visit. Winter is the perfect time to eat the famous French mountain dish raclette and even if it rains, there are many indoor activities like the new and amazing Musee de Confluence. In short, any time a year is perfect for a weekend get-away to Lyon. But even if you want to stay a week, Lyon has more than enough to offer. Just make sure to join one of our Free Walking Tours of Lyon, to get to know the city well!

What are some of the highlights of Lyon?

Lyon has many highlights. On our free walking tours of Lyon you will see many of them. There are beautifull squares like the Place de Bellecour and Place de Terreaux. There are majestic Churches like the Basilique de Fourviere and the Cathedral de St. Jean. The old neighborhood Vieux Lyon is both beautiful and very fun. The Presque'Ile is where the city center is. Here you can find great restaurants and it's the best place for shopping. And if you feel more like visiting a museum, we recommend the Musee de Confluence, a new science museum or the Musee de Beaux-Arts de Lyon, where you can find an important collection of paintings and statues.

Do you have some tips for when I visit Lyon?

On our free walking tours of Lyon, your guide will answer all your questions and give you some useful tips for your stay in Lyon, like:
  • Eat at a bouchon, a typical Lyonaise restaurant.
  • Walk up to Fourviere, for an amazing view of the city.
  • Visit Vieux Lyon at night, to find a nice restaurant or bar
  • Go up to the Roman Theatre, to enjoy some classical culture
  • Visit the Parc de Tete d'Or, with its free zoo

What books or websites can I read to prepare me for my stay in Lyon

The lonely planet guide for france is a good start with some basic information. The Only Lyon website gives you some useful information about your stay in Lyon.
