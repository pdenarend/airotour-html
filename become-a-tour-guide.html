How do you become a guide?

Become a local guide and make extra money at your own hours.

Who can become a guide?

  • Anyone living in a tourist area
  • You have to be fluent in English
  • You are passionate and know how to tell a story
  • You are a guide with experience or you are willing to learn
  • You are able to work by yourself

What are the benefits of becoming a guide?

  • You get to show people from all over your own city
  • You can determine your own hours
  • You are in charge of your own salary
  • The more you work, and the better reviews you get, the more you get to work
  • You meet people from all over the world and make them happy
